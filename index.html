In our opinion, Whole Lotta Red had a lot of great material. But it was badly ordered (no offense Kanye) and needed some substitutions. Curated by @didjesusdrop, here is our take on Whole Lotta Red, titled Whole Lotta Blue.

Whole Lotta Red tried to do two things - provide music that was an evolution of the sounds of Die Lit, and introduce a new genre that we call Punk Rap. We broke the Album into two sides to cater to this, Side A (The Day) and Side B (The Night). In the middle is the Metamorphosis, where Carti wakes from his daylight slumber and emerges as the vamp in the night. While there were other tracks we wanted to include, we wanted to keep the project as brief as possible and only include close-to-finished material.

We hope Playboi Carti still enjoyed his release and finds time to spend with his son, Onyx. Peace.
